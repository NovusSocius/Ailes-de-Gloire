History

The Saab Gripen, conceived in the late 1970s and first flown in 1988, was Sweden's answer to the need for a modern, affordable multirole fighter. Its design emphasized versatility, ease of maintenance, and digital avionics, making it one of the first fighters to successfully blend these attributes. Since its introduction in 1997, the Gripen has undergone continuous upgrades, leading to enhanced variants like the Gripen C/D and the more advanced Gripen E/F, ensuring its place in the forefront of modern military aviation.
